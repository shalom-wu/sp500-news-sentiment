# Dashboard Brief - S&P 500 News Sentiment

## Audience and purpose

This dashboard is for an interviewer or market analytics reviewer who wants to see the evidence behind the null/weak predictive result quickly.

## Pages

### 1 - Executive KPI

Cards for trading days, scored headlines, correlation to next-day return, and next-day up rate. Visuals show headline volume over time and next-day returns by sentiment bucket.

### 2 - Diagnostic Analysis

Year-level sentiment, news volume, and next-day return views. Include slicers for year and next-trading-day validity.

### 3 - Decision Support

Explain how to use the result: sentiment can help describe market narratives, but the weak forward correlation argues against treating headline tone as a simple trading rule.

## Honesty notes

Headlines are date-only, not timestamped intraday. Same-day association can be partly reverse-causal. The predictive analysis uses only valid next-trading-day pairs.
